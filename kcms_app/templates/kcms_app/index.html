Kinasang-an Case Management System

Lupon Tagapamayapa

About

A Lupon Tagapamayapa is a body of community members in each Philippine barangay that helps residents resolve disputes through amicable settlement under the Katarungang Pambarangay system.

This system centralizes KP form generation and records for faster and more organized case management by the barangay administrator.

Amicable Settlement

Encourages peaceful agreement before court litigation.

Dispute Resolution

Handles qualified minor disputes in the barangay level.

Community Harmony

Maintains good relations by resolving conflicts early.
